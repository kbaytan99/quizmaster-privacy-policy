Introduction

QuizMaster ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information Collection

What We DO NOT Collect

QuizMaster is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information:

  • No personal data (name, email, phone number)
  • No location tracking
  • No device identifiers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising IDs
  • No usage statistics
  • No internet connection required

What is Stored Locally

All data is stored only on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage):

  • Quiz scores and results
  • User preferences and settings
  • Quiz progress and statistics
  • Question reports (stored locally only)

This data never leaves your device.

Data Storage & Security

  • All quiz questions, scores, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit any data to external servers
  • No cloud backup or synchronization
  • Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

QuizMaster does not use any third-party services:

  • No analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media integration

Permissions

QuizMaster requires no special permissions:

  • No internet access required
  • No camera access
  • No location access
  • No storage access (beyond app data)

The app is fully functional offline.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is designed for educational purposes and is safe for all ages.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All data is stored locally on your device
  • Delete: Uninstall the app or clear app data in Android settings
  • Export: All data is stored in standard formats on your device
  • No tracking: We cannot track you because we don't collect any data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Continued use of QuizMaster after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

  • Data is stored on your device until you delete it
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data
  • We do not retain any data on servers (because we don't have any servers)

Summary

QuizMaster is a privacy-first application:

  • No data collection
  • All data stored locally on your device
  • No internet required
  • No tracking or analytics
  • Complete privacy

Your data is yours. We can't access it, we don't want it, and we don't need it.
